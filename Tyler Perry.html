TYLER PERRY American Actor

Hello, my name is Tyler. I'm an American actor, writer, producer, comedian and director. In 2011, Forbes listed me as the highest-paid man in entertainment, earning US$130 million between May 2010 and May 2011. My films vary in style from orthodox filmmaking techniques to filmed productions of live stage plays.
